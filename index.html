We are one of the most progressive boatyards in Scotland with a highly skilled and motivated workforce.

Our wide skill base with ISO9001: 2000 qualification and continuous training policy ensures that quality remains our prime concern.

We can provide the skills and materials necessary to keep your boat in top condition.

We are located on the Rosneath Peninsula and situated at the picturesque and sheltered Stroul Bay on the west shore of the Gareloch.
